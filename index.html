Sentinel MP is the flagship environmental monitoring platform from Pomona Systems — purpose-built for controlled-atmosphere apple storage.

Designed to sample oxygen (O₂) and carbon dioxide (CO₂) across up to 10 rooms from a single unified system, Sentinel MP delivers dependable measurements when fruit quality, storage life, and safety depend on precision.

Developed by a grower who understands the realities of CA storage, Sentinel MP focuses on clarity, reliability, and control — without the complexity of legacy gas analyzers.

Operate fully standalone with on-device visibility and secure local logging, or connect to the cloud for remote access and historical tracking across your facility.
